About Us

BizSci: Open Source Software for Business & Financial Analysis

Our Mission

To provide the fastest, most scalable, and easiest to use R packages focused on solving business and financial analysis problems.


Our goal is to solve problems by creating “a better way.” We develop professional-grade, open-source software for use with the R statistical programming language.

Business Science
Open Source Software for Business & Financial Analysis

Our Motivation

During an analytical endeavour, have you ever asked yourself, "Is there a better way?"


We believe there is, and it involves the R Programming Language.

We provide solutions in the realm of business and financial analysis through the development of fast, scalable and user-friendly open-source R software.

We seek to assist users in their transition to R. Our focus is on users that currently perform business and financial analytics using:

  • SAS statistcal software
  • Stata
  • Microsoft Excel
  • Any other analytics solution that is not RLang

We believe R is the right analytics approach for several reasons:

  • Open-source
  • Over 10,000 packages employing the latest statistical & visualization techniques
  • Integrated tools for reporting including Rmarkdown and Shiny
  • An active and welcoming R Community
  • Highly scalable: Easily apply an analysis to many groups
