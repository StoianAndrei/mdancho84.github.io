Financial Analytics

We specialize in advanced algorithms, portfolio analysis and optimization, predictive analytics, data visualization and more all within the area of finance and risk management. Our primary package is tidyquant developed in-house with flexibility and scaleability in mind.

Advanced Forecasting

We utilize the latest techniques in forecasting to develop superior time series models and predictive analytics by identifying patterns in your data. We employ several primary packages including forecast and prophet along with our own in-house package timekit.

Anomaly Detection

Time series anomalies can help in understanding user behavior or be used to warn analysts of an issue. We implement algorithms that detect anomalies as they occur. Further, with our full stack web services we can create alert systems so your analysts can investigate anomalies before they turn into a problem!

Event Impact

Measure the impact of an event on a time series. We utilize the latest techniques including Google's CausalInference package, which implements Bayesian methods to measure point impact and cummulative effect. Perfect for determining the effects of marketing on customer behavior or external events such has oil prices on corporate revenue!
