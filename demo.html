Customer Analytics: Predict Customer Churn

Use machine learning to predict customer churn

and to make recommendations on how to improve customer health


Problem

A major issue for an organization is losing customers. But, what if the at-risk customers could be targeted prior to leaving?

Solution

Machine learning is a perfect solution. We build high accuracy ML models to identify customers at risk and explain what factors lead to turnover. The end result is the ability to improve customer health before customers leave.

Communication

The organization can then make decisions based on the model. We build applications to distribute analytics, which empowers Customer Analytics Professionals to use the model to target at-risk customers.

Communication
