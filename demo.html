Customer Analytics: Reduce Customer Churn

Use machine learning to make recommendations

on how to improve customer health


Problem

Customer churn is analagous to filling a water bucket with a bunch of holes. If you cant plug the holes, your bucket is not going to fill up.

Solution

Machine learning (Deep Learning with an Artificial Neural Network) is a highly accurate solution that enables evaluating customer health before customers leave.

Communication

The Customer Scorecard is a way to communicate the Deep Learning model in a way that decision makers can easily digest. The end result is that insights can be worked into decision making process.
